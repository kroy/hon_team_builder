HoN Lineup Builder beta

This is the Smells Like Fail Heroes of Newerth Lineup Builder! You can build a team lineup here, share it with your friends, and allow them all to laugh at you. Eventually, you'll be able to add notes, add suggested item builds, add alternate heroes, perform mock captain's mode drafts and more! But for now, we're in development, and this is Beta, so be gentle! Email any bugs/suggestions/comments/hate to: krakensmellsfail@gmail.com

Instructions: To add a hero to the lineup, left click on his/her portrait in the hero board. Heroes will be added to the first available slot. To remove a hero from your lineup, just double-click on their portrait, or click the ✖ below their portrait. To share with your friends, click the "Generate URL" button and copy the address in your browser window. Pretttaaaayyyyy simple.
